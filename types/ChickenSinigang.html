Chicken Sinigang!!!

How to cook from scractch

I usually use packaged tamarind mixes as the fresh fruit is not always available in my area. Although these powder flavorings are easy and convenient to use, nothing beats pork sinigang from scratch! Just follow the steps below on how to use green tamarind pods.

  1. Heat oil in a saucepan. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken once the onion softens. Continue cooking until the color of the chicken turns light brown.
  3. Pour water. Let it boil. Add Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc with Gabi Recipe Mix and daikon radish. Cover and continue boiling using low heat for 40 minutes.
  4. Add string beans, long green pepper, okra, and tomato. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Season with fish sauce and ground white pepper. Transfer to a serving bowl. Share and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Gabi
  • Radish
  • Vegetables
  • Tamarind Powder
